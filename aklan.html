Aklan

Home of Boracay — but also home to culture, tradition, and peaceful provincial life outside the island.

Boracay brings the energy: nightlife, powder-white sand, and sunsets that feel unreal. But Aklan beyond Boracay? Quiet towns, rivers, local markets — a mix of adventure and chill moments.
