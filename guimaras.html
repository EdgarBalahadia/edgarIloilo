Guimaras

Famous for the sweetest mangoes in the Philippines — pero hindi lang mangga ang bida dito.

Beaches, rolling hills, peaceful roads perfect for island motorbike rides, and simple life vibes everywhere. Guimaras feels warm — not just because of the sun but because the place is calm, humble, and welcoming.
