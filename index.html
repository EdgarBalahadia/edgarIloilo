Welcome to Iloilo

Where warm smiles, heritage charm, and delicious street nights come alive.

Discover Iloilo

Iloilo is a city that glows with heart — from its Spanish-era plazas and graceful churches to energetic food streets and peaceful night walks by the riverside.

Iloilo Night Market

The city’s midnight playground — smoky grills sizzling, locals crowding long tables, laughter echoing down the street. Here, you don’t just eat. You feel Iloilo’s heartbeat.

Iloilo Plaza

Lanterns glowing, music playing softly, families strolling, couples sharing quiet moments. The plaza is where life slows down and Iloilo’s charm whispers to you.
