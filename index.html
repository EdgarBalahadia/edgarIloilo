Welcome to Iloilo

The City of Love — where culture, flavors, and warm smiles gather.

Discover Iloilo

Iloilo is a city of vibrant food streets, historic Spanish-era plazas, and scenic night spots where locals and travelers gather to eat, laugh, and enjoy the warm Ilonggo charm.

Iloilo Night Market

A lively night-time food haven filled with sizzling BBQ stalls, street vendors, and crowds enjoying late-night eats until midnight.

Iloilo Plaza

The heart of Iloilo — glowing church lights, festive energy, and families enjoying peaceful night walks by the historic plaza.
