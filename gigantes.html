Gigantes Island

Hidden paradise in Northern Iloilo — crystal waters, dramatic cliffs, and untouched island serenity.

Island Adventure Awaits

Gigantes Island is where raw nature meets quiet escape. Limestone cliffs, turquoise lagoons, powdery sandbars, and seafood feasts — not crowded, just pure tropical peace and adventure.

Pulupandan Island

A tiny gem in the middle of the sea — just sand, a lone coconut tree, and peace. The perfect island fantasy made real.

Tangke Saltwater Lagoon

A natural pool tucked between dramatic limestone cliffs — clear emerald water perfect for a refreshing dip. Feels like stepping into a secret fantasy world.

Cabugao Gamay Island

The postcard star of Gigantes — white sandbar, turquoise water, and iconic viewpoint. Calm sunrise, dreamy afternoons, breathtaking views everywhere.
