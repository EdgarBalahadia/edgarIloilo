Gigantes Island

Raw island beauty — dramatic cliffs, serene lagoons, and crystal-clear dreams.

Northern Iloilo's Hidden Treasure

Imagine sailing into a world where limestone giants rise over turquoise seas — where time slows, waves sparkle, and seafood feasts welcome every traveler. That's Gigantes: untouched, enchanting, and unforgettable.

Pulupandan Island

A tiny sandcastle in the middle of a vast sea — quiet, dreamy, and kissed by gentle waves. One coconut tree, sparkling waters, endless sky. The ultimate escape for soul-deep calm.

Tangke Saltwater Lagoon

A secret emerald pool surrounded by towering cliffs — nature’s hidden bath. Calm waters, dancing sunlight, and a magical stillness that feels unreal. Dip, float, breathe, repeat.

Cabugao Gamay Island

The crown jewel of Gigantes — iconic viewpoints, sweeping sandbars, and that postcard moment you’ll remember forever. Climb, gaze, breathe in the island magic.
